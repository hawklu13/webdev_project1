My Favorite Players

    List and descriptions of my favorite players.
  • Derrick Rose
      • Derrick Rose is my favorite basketball player of all time. Hes very flashy and fun to watch. He always finds a way to get to the rim and lay up the ball. Hes very fast and I love the fact he is from chicago. hes a hometown hero of sorts. I wish his career didnt get cut short. He is chicagos MVP.
  • Demar Derozan
      • Demar was traded to the bulls in the 2021 season. He surprised everyone with how good of a scorer and how clutch he was. He won us a lot of games with his clutch ability. He got traded not too long ago, but I am happy he played for us and he can thrive on his new team.
  • Matas Buzelis
      • Matas Buzelis is a rookie, just got drafted this past year. He is from the suburbs of chicago so I like that about him. Hes a great dunker and plays really good defense. Hope he can show us some more of his potential soon.
